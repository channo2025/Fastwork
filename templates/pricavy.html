Privacy Policy

Simple, transparent information about how JobDash handles basic data.

JobDash is a simple beta platform. We currently store only the information you enter when you post a job (title, category, location, pay and description). This is used only to display tasks to workers on the site.

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with advertisers. In a future version, this page will be updated with more details as we add accounts, payments or messaging.
