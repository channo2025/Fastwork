Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

1) What we collect

Win-Win Job is a digital job center. We may collect basic information you submit on the site, such as: your name, phone number, email (optional), job postings content (title, city, pay, description), and messages you write.

2) What we do NOT want

Do not submit sensitive personal data (SSN, bank info, passwords, immigration documents). If you share it, you do so at your own risk.

3) How we use info

  • To publish job listings and help users browse jobs.
  • To allow workers to apply and employers to review applicants (demo mode now; features may evolve).
  • To improve site performance, security, and user experience.

4) Cookies / analytics

We may use basic cookies for functionality and security. If we add analytics later, we will update this policy.

5) Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited information only if required by law, to prevent fraud/abuse, or to protect users and the platform.

6) Safety

Win-Win Job is a marketplace connector. Always verify pay and meeting location. Meet in public places. Use caution.

Questions? Use the Contact page.
