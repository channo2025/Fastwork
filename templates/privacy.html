Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-01-01 (update anytime later).

JobDash is a local job marketplace. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information we collect

  • Contact details you provide (such as email or phone number) when posting a job or contacting support.
  • Job details you submit (title, description, location, budget).
  • Basic technical data (browser type, device, and standard server logs) for security and analytics.

How we use information

  • To operate the website and display job listings.
  • To respond to support requests and improve the service.
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, or security issues.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information if required by law, or to protect JobDash and users from fraud or harm.

Your choices

  • You can request deletion of a job listing by contacting support.
  • You can stop using the service at any time.

Note: For V1, “Apply” may contact the job poster directly (email/WhatsApp). Please avoid sharing sensitive personal information.

By using JobDash, you consent to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
