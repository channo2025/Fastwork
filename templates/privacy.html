  • To display job posts on the platform.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information. Job information you submit (title, location, description) is visible publicly so workers can find and apply to tasks.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no online platform can guarantee 100% security.
