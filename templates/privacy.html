Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how JobDash Online (“we”, “our”, “the Platform”) collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Basic details you submit when posting or applying to a job (title, description, location, etc.).
  • No account creation is required, and we do not collect passwords.
  • We do not collect financial or payment information.

2. How Your Information Is Used

We only use the information you provide to display job listings publicly so workers can find them. We do not sell or share data with third parties.

3. Cookies & Analytics

We may use simple analytics tools to understand traffic and improve the platform. No personal identity is tracked.

4. Data Protection

We take reasonable steps to keep your information safe. However, since JobDash is a simple listing platform, we recommend not sharing sensitive information in job descriptions.

5. Contact

If you have questions, email us at: hello@jobdashonline.com
