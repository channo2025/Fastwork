Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-12-17

This Privacy Policy explains how JobChap collects, uses, and shares information when you use our platform.

1) Information we collect

  • Information you provide: name, email, messages, job postings, and application messages.
  • Basic technical data: device/browser info, IP address, and logs for security and debugging.
  • Cookies: we may use cookies for basic site functionality and analytics (if enabled).

2) How we use information

  • To operate the platform (post jobs, submit applications, respond to messages).
  • To improve safety, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot issues.
  • To communicate with you about important updates.

3) Sharing

We may share information with service providers (hosting, analytics) only as needed to run the site. We may disclose information if required by law, to protect users, or to prevent fraud/abuse.

4) Data retention

We keep data only as long as necessary for platform operations, security, and legal compliance. We may delete data periodically.

5) Security

We use reasonable safeguards, but no system is 100% secure. Use the platform at your own risk and avoid posting sensitive data.

6) Children

JobChap is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your region). Do not use the site if you do not meet the legal age requirement.

7) Your choices

You can request deletion of messages you submitted by contacting us. We may need to retain certain records for legal or security reasons.

8) Contact

For privacy requests, contact us via Contact.
