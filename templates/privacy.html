Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how JobDash Online ("we", "us", or "the Platform") handles information when you use our website and services.

1. Information we collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information – name, email, and basic contact details if you create an account in the future.
  • Job information – details about tasks you post or apply to (location area, category, description, pay).
  • Technical information – basic usage data such as pages visited, browser type, and approximate location (city/region).

2. How we use your information

We use this information to:

  • Show and manage job posts on the Platform.
  • Help connect Employers and Workers.
  • Improve the Platform and fix technical issues.
  • Communicate important updates about the service.

3. Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information only when necessary:

  • With service providers who help us operate the Platform (for example, hosting or email tools).
  • If required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • To protect the rights, safety, or property of JobDash Online or other users.

4. Cookies and basic analytics

The Platform may use simple cookies or analytics tools to understand how users navigate the site and to improve the experience. You can usually control cookies from your browser settings.

5. Data security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no online service is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data retention

We keep information only as long as necessary to operate the Platform, meet legal obligations, or resolve disputes. Job information may be kept for a limited period for record and safety reasons.

7. Your choices

You may contact us if you want to:

  • Update or correct your contact information.
  • Request deletion of certain information, where possible.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at:
hello@jobdashonline.com

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make important changes, we will update the date at the bottom of this page.
