How does JobDash work?

JobDash is a digital work portal that lets people find help or find work instantly. Like a modern job center — but 100% online, fast and easy.

For workers

  • Create a profile in minutes
  • Choose the tasks you can do
  • Pick nearby jobs
  • Contact clients or get contacted
  • Get paid instantly

For clients

  • Post a task in 1 minute
  • Choose a nearby worker
  • Set budget & time
  • Get help the same day
