How does JobDash work?

JobDash is a digital work portal that makes it simple for people to find help or find work instantly. It functions like a modern job center — but 100% online, fast and easy.

For workers

Earn money quickly by doing simple tasks in your area.

You can:

  • Create a profile in minutes
  • Choose the tasks you can do
  • Pick small jobs near you
  • Contact clients or wait for clients to contact you
  • Get paid right after the work

Common tasks include:

  • Cleaning
  • Moving help
  • Laundry & folding
  • Dishwashing
  • Yard work
  • Shopping errands
  • Babysitting
  • Small repairs
  • And more

Quick jobs. Real money.

For clients

Get reliable help fast.

You can:

  • Post a job in less than 1 minute
  • Choose a worker available nearby
  • Set your budget and time
  • Receive help the same day

Examples:

  • “Help me move 6 boxes”
  • “Clean my kitchen”
  • “Rake my yard and take trash out”
