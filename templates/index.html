How does JobDash work?

JobDash is a digital work portal that makes it simple for people to find help or find work instantly. Like a modern job center — but 100% online, fast and easy.

For workers

Earn money by doing simple tasks in your area.

  • Create a profile in minutes
  • Choose the tasks you can do
  • Pick jobs near you
  • Contact clients or get contacted
  • Get paid instantly

Popular tasks:

  • Cleaning & dishwashing
  • Moving help
  • Laundry & folding
  • Yard work
  • Babysitting
