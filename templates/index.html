How does JobDash work?

JobDash is a digital work portal that lets people find help or find work instantly. Like a modern job center — but 100% online, fast and easy.

For workers

  • Create a simple profile in minutes
  • Choose the tasks you can do
  • Pick jobs near your area
  • Contact clients or get contacted
  • Get paid quickly after the work

For clients

  • Post a task in less than 1 minute
  • Choose a local worker who can help
  • Set your budget and time
  • Get help the same day for everyday tasks
