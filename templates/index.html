How does FastWork work?

FastWork is a digital platform that connects people who need help with reliable workers available for quick tasks.

For clients

Get help quickly for everyday tasks.

  • Post a task: cleaning, moving help, gardening, assembly, etc.
  • Set the location, schedule, and budget.
  • Browse nearby worker profiles and pick the right person.
  • The task gets done at the requested time.

For workers

Earn money on your own schedule.

  • Create your profile in a few minutes.
  • Select your skills and the area where you can work.
  • Receive task requests near you on the platform.
  • Accept the job, complete it, and get paid.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is FastWork a job center?

No. FastWork is not a physical job center. It is a digital platform where everything happens online, from posting tasks to finding workers.

Who can use FastWork?

Everyone. Clients, workers, students, families, freelancers — anyone who needs help or wants to earn extra money with simple tasks.

Do I need a resume?

No. You can start receiving task requests without a resume or formal interview. Your profile, skills, and reviews will help clients choose you.
