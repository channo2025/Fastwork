How does FastWork work?

FastWork is a digital platform that connects people who need help with reliable workers available for quick tasks.

For clients

  • Post a task in less than two minutes.
  • Set your location, time and budget.
  • Choose a worker from nearby profiles.
  • Get the job done when you need it.

For workers

  • Create your profile and select your skills.
  • Receive task requests in your area.
  • Accept only the jobs you want.
  • Complete the task and get paid.
