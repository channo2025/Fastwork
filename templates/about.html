About JobDash Online

JobDash Online is a simple job center on the internet where people can post small local tasks and find workers ready to earn quick cash. No resume, no interview â€“ just clear tasks and direct payment between people.

Our mission

Our mission is to make it easier for:

  • People who need help with everyday tasks
  • Workers who want to earn extra money quickly and flexibly

We want to connect local communities, help newcomers, students and part-time workers find opportunities, and make small jobs easier to organize.

Who is JobDash for?

  • Busy parents who need help with cleaning or shopping
  • Students or newcomers looking for fast side jobs
  • People who need help moving, yard work, or small home tasks
  • Anyone who wants to post a simple, short-term job

How JobDash works

  1. The Employer posts a local job on JobDash (for example: cleaning, moving, yard work).
  2. Workers can view available jobs and decide which tasks match their skills.
  3. Employer and Worker contact each other directly to confirm details.
  4. Payment is handled directly between them after the job is done (cash or preferred method).

What JobDash is not

JobDash Online is not an employer and does not hire workers. We do not manage contracts, payment methods, or job performance. All agreements are directly between the people using the platform.

Future plans

In the future, we plan to add:

  • User accounts for Employers and Workers
  • Notifications and better job matching
  • More safety and verification tools

JobDash Online is still in its early version, but we are working step by step to make it a trusted place for quick local jobs.
