About JobChap

A digital jobcenter platform built to connect workers and clients for fast local tasks.

JobChap helps people find quick, local work (and helps clients get help fast). We’re a marketplace-style platform: workers choose tasks, clients post needs.

What JobChap is

  • A digital jobcenter for short tasks
  • A place to discover and post local work
  • A platform that helps people move faster (same-day/next-day)

What JobChap is not

  • We are not an employer, staffing agency, or background-check company
  • We don’t guarantee earnings, hiring, or job availability
