About JobDash

JobDash is an online jobcenter for quick, local tasks – created from real experience in the community.

JobDash was created after seeing how many people wait for hours at worker centers hoping for a small job, while many families and small businesses need help but don't know where to find reliable workers.

Our mission is simple: make it easier for people to find quick work and for clients to get help with everyday tasks – without resumes, interviews or complicated applications.

JobDash is especially helpful for newcomers, immigrants, students and anyone who needs flexible, same-day work to support their life in the U.S.
