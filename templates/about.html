About JobChap

JobChap is a digital job marketplace designed to connect people who need quick local help with workers ready to earn money fast.

From cleaning and moving to yard work, shopping, and small tasks, JobChap makes it easy to post a job and get help the same day.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: help people work today and get paid today. We believe access to quick work opportunities should be easy, fair, and transparent.

JobChap & Jobcenter

JobChap is a digital platform inspired by real-world job centers such as Vozw Workers. While traditional job centers operate physically, JobChap brings the job center experience online, making it accessible anytime and anywhere.

JobChap operates as an independent digital platform and does not act as an employer, recruiter, or staffing agency.

How It Works

  • Clients post short-term jobs.
  • Workers browse available tasks.
  • Both parties connect directly.

JobChap does not supervise, manage, or guarantee any job or worker. All agreements are made directly between users.
