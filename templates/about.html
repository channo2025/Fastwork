About JobDash

JobDash is a simple online jobcenter for everyday tasks like cleaning, moving help, yard work and shopping. It is designed for newcomers, students and busy families who need fast help and prefer flexible work.

Our goal is to make it easy for people to:

  • Post small local jobs in a few clicks;
  • Find quick work without a long CV or interview;
  • Get paid directly by the client (cash or app) once the job is done.

JobDash is not an employer. We simply connect people who need help with local independent workers.
